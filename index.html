I am a DPhil student in Department of Computer Science, University of Oxford, also a member of St Hugh's College. I am co-supervised by Prof. Andrew Markham and Prof. Niki Trigoni.

Prior to Oxford, I worked as a research scientist at Malong Technologies, self-driving engineer at Baidu Institute of Deep Learning (IDL). I received B.Eng. in photogrammetry and remote sensing from Wuhan University, China.

My research interest broadly lies in computer vision, audio signal processing and machine learning. Specifically, I am working on recovering geometry information from various sensors.
